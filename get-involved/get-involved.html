Become a Mentor

Give students real-world guidance and help them build confidence for what is next.

  • Flexible time commitment
  • Virtual and in-person options
  • Support from Sac Pathways team

Purpose & Impact

Mentors and volunteers are the heart of Sac Pathways. By sharing your time, experience, and perspective, you help students gain confidence, explore opportunities, and navigate important academic and career decisions.

What Participation Looks Like

Whether you are offering one-on-one mentorship, supporting events, or helping behind the scenes, your involvement is flexible and meaningful. We work with you to match your skills, availability, and interests with the needs of our students and programs.

Invitation & Reassurance

No prior mentoring experience is required -- just a genuine interest in making a positive impact. Complete the form to tell us how you would like to get involved, and our team will follow up to guide you through the next steps.

What happens next

We review applications within 5-7 business days. Approved mentors are invited to a short onboarding call. Expect an email from sac.pathways@gmail.com.
